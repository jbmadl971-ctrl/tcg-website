About

I'm Jon Madl. I diagnose, then I connect, then I build.

Founder

Built from the inside out.

I didn't start in a consulting firm. I started inside businesses that needed to actually work — managing operations across 90+ locations, taking a single store from $90K to $2.6M in 18 months during a pandemic, and learning what actually drives growth versus what just looks like growth on a slide deck.

That's the gap TCG closes. We come in, see the whole system, and fix what's actually broken — not what the owner thinks is broken, which is usually a symptom of something deeper.

I use AI to move faster and see patterns at scale — but this work is fundamentally relational. The technology amplifies the judgment. It doesn't replace it.
