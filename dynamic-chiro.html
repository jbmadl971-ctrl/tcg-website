About Dr. Grissette

Chiropractic care that actually fits your life.

Dr. Jordan Grissette started Dynamic Chiropractic because he saw a gap: people in real pain who needed real help — but the system made it harder, not easier. Scheduling delays. Commutes. Assembly-line appointments where you got 10 minutes if you were lucky.

Mobile chiropractic was the answer. Every patient gets Dr. Grissette's full focus, in their own environment, on their own schedule. That's the model — and it works.

With a background in sports performance and a decade spent working alongside athletes and active families, Dr. Grissette brings both clinical precision and real-world practicality to every visit. He specializes in spinal dysfunction, sports recovery, and prenatal care — and he believes strongly that chiropractic works best when patients aren't stressed, rushed, or fighting traffic to get there.
